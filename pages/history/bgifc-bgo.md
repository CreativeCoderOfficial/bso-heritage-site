## British Guides in Foreign Countries (BGIFC) & British Guides Overseas (BGO)

    <strong>BGIFC Years Active:</strong> 1911 - 2017 | <strong>BGO Years Active:</strong> 2017 - Present

### Summary

The British Guides in Foreign Countries (BGIFC) was the guiding counterpart to BTIFC, established in 1911 to provide Guiding opportunities for British girls and women living abroad. For over a century, BGIFC supported the Guiding movement across international British communities.

In 2017, BGIFC was restructured and renamed as British Guides Overseas (BGO), maintaining the same mission of supporting British Guiding worldwide while adopting a more modern name that reflected the organisation's global scope.
