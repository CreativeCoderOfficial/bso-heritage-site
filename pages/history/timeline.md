## Chronological Timeline

### Early Years (1908-1946)

**1908** — Scout Movement founded by Robert Baden-Powell

**1909** — <span class="badge bg-success">Formation</span> BTIFC (British Troops in Foreign Countries) formed | <a href="{{ '/history/btifc.html' | relative_url }}" class="text-decoration-none">View organisation</a>

**1911** — <span class="badge bg-success">Formation</span> BGIFC (British Guides in Foreign Countries) formed | <a href="{{ '/history/bgifc-bgo.html' | relative_url }}" class="text-decoration-none">View organisation</a>

### Post-War Expansion (1947-1951)

**1947** — <span class="badge bg-success">Formation</span> BSG (British Scouts in Germany) created | <a href="{{ '/history/bsg.html' | relative_url }}" class="text-decoration-none">View organisation</a>

### Reorganization Period (1952)

**1952** — <span class="badge bg-warning text-dark">Split</span> BTIFC splits into BGA (British Groups Abroad) + BSG | <a href="{{ '/history/british-groups-abroad.html' | relative_url }}" class="text-decoration-none">BGA</a>, <a href="{{ '/history/bsg.html' | relative_url }}" class="text-decoration-none">BSG</a>

**1952** — <span class="badge bg-success">Formation</span> BSWE (British Scouting in Western Europe) formed | <a href="{{ '/history/bswe-germany-europe.html' | relative_url }}" class="text-decoration-none">View organisation</a>

### Modern Era

**2013** — <span class="badge bg-info text-dark">Merge</span> BGA + BSWE merge into BSO (British Scouting Overseas) | <a href="{{ '/history/bso-2013-present.html' | relative_url }}" class="text-decoration-none">View organisation</a>

**2017** — <span class="badge bg-success">Formation</span> BGO (British Guides Overseas) established | <a href="{{ '/history/bgifc-bgo.html' | relative_url }}" class="text-decoration-none">View organisation</a>
