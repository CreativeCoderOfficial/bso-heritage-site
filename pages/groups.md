## About BSO Groups

British Scouting Overseas Groups follow the same programme as UK Scouts, adapted for their international context. Groups typically meet weekly and participate in local, national, and international events.

Each Group is run by dedicated adult volunteers who deliver a high-quality Scouting experience for young people aged 6-18, following the UK Scout Association's programme for Beavers, Cubs, Scouts, and Explorers.
