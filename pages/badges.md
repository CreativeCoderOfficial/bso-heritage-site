## BSO Badge Collection

Badges represent the achievements and skills development of Scouts in the British Scouting Overseas programme. This collection includes historical badges from the British Groups Abroad and BSWE eras, as well as current BSO programme badges.

Each badge tells a story of learning, perseverance, and the international spirit of Scouting.
