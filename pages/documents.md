## Historical Archive

The BSO document collection includes a wide range of materials that tell the story of British Scouting abroad:

- Annual reports and meeting minutes
- Newsletters and publications
- Event programmes and camp records
- Administrative documents and correspondence
- Historical photographs and ephemera

These documents provide invaluable insights into the development and evolution of British Scouting communities worldwide.
